About GETIIT

A people-first IT consultancy serving global education and enterprise.

Founded in 2007, we've been delivering transformation through technology across Australia, Asia Pacific, and beyond. Our strength lies in our team.

Who we are

"Global Electronic Transformation with Innovative IT Services Company"

GETIIT was founded in 2007, and has since been providing significant information technology and virtual learning services to our clients.

GETIIT is headquartered in Sydney and operates across locations globally. We are, above all, a people company. Our operations are spread across Australia and the Asia Pacific region. Management and support roles aside, our employees are grouped into IT Consulting, Virtual Learning and Co-Sourcing, each governed by its own specific economic rules and managed with its own profit.

Our values: working together

"Collaboration is at the heart of every interaction with our clients"

GETIIT consultants listen carefully to understand your situation before suggesting a solution. We work with you to develop a clear path to realistic project goals. By sharing knowledge effectively, we make life simpler.

Unique challenges deserve unique solutions. Our way of working with our clients, called Customer Business Experience, brings you greater flexibility, reactivity and creativity — qualities that elevate your performance.

A study begins with a discussion of the questions to be addressed and prioritisation. We lend our expertise to design and blueprint, to optimally address questions and maximise time and cost efficiency.

Our professional expertise and extensive market knowledge provide a strong foundation to guide you through transformation. GETIIT is with you every step of the way, giving you the flexibility to focus your energies where they are most effective.

We work closely with our education sector customers to develop study documentation and statistical analysis plans, so the delivered results can be integrated into sponsor documentation and statistical analysis.

GETIIT implements high quality control on all data, incorporating objective methods to identify issues. Sites are monitored on an ongoing basis, with sponsor visibility to queries and resolution of issues.

Our processing and analysis steps incorporate years of advances in optimising education ERP implementation and support, producing interpretable results for our sponsors.

Best practices in project management are followed, adhering to quality standards such as ISO 9001. Updates and interim reports are provided, alongside project plans, schedule plans and task masters. Presentation and publication support is provided according to the needs of our sponsors.
